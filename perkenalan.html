      Semeru Mountain

      Jawa Timur

      What?

      Mount Semeru or Mount Meru is a cone volcano in East Java, Indonesia. Mount Semeru is the highest mountain on the island of Java, with its peak Mahameru, 3,676 meters above sea level (masl).

      Where?

      Malang Regency and Lumajang Regency, East Java Province. This mountain is included in the Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park area.

      Price?

      Each person who wants to climb is charged Rp. 17,500 per person per day for weekdays, and Rp. 22,500 per person per day for holidays.




      Arjuno Mountain

      Jawa Timur

      What?

      Mount Arjuno is the fourth highest on Java Island. Usually this mountain is reached from three well-known climbing points namely from Lawang, Tretes and Batu.

      Where?

      Cone volcano in East Java, Indonesia with an altitude of 3333 m above sea level. Mount Arjuno is administratively located on the border of Batu City.

      Price?

      HTM in Pet Leaks Rp.10,000 per day, but when I pass by there is no charge at the Leaked Pet Post.
